    ABOUT SUPER NINJA WARRIORS GAME :
    • You are a Super Hero. Play as a Super Ninja Warrior with Super powers like Thunder power, Fireball power, Ritual Flame power and 3 types of Sword Attack. Your goal in this game is to Save the World from Evil's like Zombie's, Shadowkon's, Skeleton King and finally the Lord Of Evil ( Evil Boss ). In order to save the world you must find the Load Of Evil and kill him by using all your super powers.
    • This Game consists of two phases Rescue Town and Graveyard Clash.
    • In Rescue Town, a full city will be haunted by Evil Zombies, Evil Shadowcon's , Skeleton King you must fight with all of them and finally reach the Lord of Evil and kill him to Save the haunted city back to normal.
    • In Graveyard Clash, there will huge amount of Evils waiting for a rival battle, You must fight with all those evils using your super powers and finally kill the Lord of Evil to win the battle and save the world.
